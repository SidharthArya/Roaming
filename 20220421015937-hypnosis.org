* Hypnotic Induction
:PROPERTIES:
:ID:       4e49b7d2-0af8-42f3-9b72-c5b16e25ef1f
:END:
Hypnotic induction is the process undertaken by a hypnotist to establish the state or conditions required for hypnosis to occur.
